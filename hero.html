About This Project

Spirits of the Continent is a digital museum dedicated to preserving and celebrating the rich mythology of Sub-Saharan Africa. This catalogue features deities, spirits, tricksters, and legendary beings from across the continent, from the Yoruba pantheon of Nigeria to the ancestral spirits of the Zulu.

Our mission is to make African mythology accessible to everyone, honoring the oral traditions and cultural heritage that have been passed down through generations. Each entry in this catalogue represents a window into the spiritual beliefs and values of African communities.

Browse through our collection, learn about different cultures, and discover the fascinating beings that have shaped the spiritual landscape of Africa for centuries.
